California Roll

California roll, a type of inside-out sushi roll (uramaki) in which vinegared rice (rather than nori, an edible seaweed) forms the outside of the roll, usually encompassing cucumber, crab (or imitation crab), and avocado. The rice is often topped with sesame seeds, tobiko (eggs of the flying fish), or masago (eggs of the capelin, a species of smelt).
